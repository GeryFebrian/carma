About Us

Carma E-Commerce, founded in 2024 and headquartered in Indonesia, is dedicated to empowering your journey with green energy. Our mission is to support a zero-emission lifestyle for a better, more sustainable future. We believe in the power of clean energy and are committed to providing our customers with eco-friendly solutions that don’t compromise on quality or convenience. Join us in our journey towards a greener planet.

Vision & Mission

Vision

  • To be a leading provider of electric vehicles in Indonesia and beyond.
  • Drive the transition to sustainable transportation by offering affordable and high-quality electric vehicles.
  • Create a future where travel doesn't compromise the health of our planet.
  • Empower customers to make eco-friendly choices without sacrificing convenience or performance.

Mission

  • Offer a wide range of electric vehicles to cater to the diverse needs of our customers.
  • Provide comprehensive support for charging stations, ensuring seamless and worry-free travel for our customers.
  • Make electric vehicles accessible to all by offering competitive pricing.
  • Deliver exceptional customer service that matches the quality of our vehicles.
  • Continuously innovate and improve, staying at the forefront of green technology.
